# The Workplace Setting:

Generate a compelling workplace setting for a psychological thriller. The environment should be realistic but contain inherent tensions, power dynamics, and opportunities for paranoia.

## Use this schema:
~~~
## Setup - Workplace Setting:
The setting is: {setting}

## Evaluation:
- Is this setting believable and tense?
- What is one suggestion to improve it?
~~~

---

### Example:
~~~
## Setup - Workplace Setting:
The setting is: A high-pressure tech startup with glass-walled offices, ambient surveillance cameras, and whispered rumors about product failures leaking to the press.

## Evaluation:
- Believability and tension: High tension; surveillance creates realistic unease.
- Suggestion: Add detail about how employees cope with constant monitoring.
~~~
